Questions

Asked and answered.

Do I need a new number or WhatsApp Business API?
No. You connect your existing WhatsApp number with a QR scan — the same way WhatsApp Web works. No API application, no waiting for approval, no per-message Meta fees.
Will my customers know it's an AI?
That's up to you. You write the AI's instructions — many businesses have it introduce itself as an assistant, others keep it seamless. Either way, replies use your tone and only your facts.
What happens when a customer needs a real person?
The AI steps aside automatically when a customer uses your handoff keywords (like "agent" or "complaint"), and you can reply yourself in the same inbox at any moment. You're always in control.
Is automating my WhatsApp number safe?
We design for safety: human-like reply delays, hourly limits, and no bulk blasting. That said, QR-based automation isn't officially endorsed by WhatsApp, so we recommend starting with a dedicated business number — and we'll always be straight with you about this.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — one click from your billing page, no calls, no emails. Your chat history stays safe.
